IllumiSeg: Illumination-Degradation-Aware Polyp Segmentation

Medical AI • Deep Learning

End-to-end deep learning model that jointly enhances illumination and segments polyps in a single forward pass, addressing the critical problem of dark-frame polyp detection in capsule endoscopy where up to 26% of polyps are missed.

  • Introduces Retinex Feature Disentanglement Module (RFDM) separating encoder features into illumination and tissue streams in feature space.
  • Per-pixel uncertainty map highlights dark regions and amplifies gradients during training, forcing focus on frames where existing models fail.
  • Built on MiT-B2 encoder with enhancement decoder active only during training — zero inference overhead.
  • Evaluated on 6 public datasets including Kvasir-SEG, CVC-ClinicDB, and cross-domain generalization tests.
